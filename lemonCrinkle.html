Lemon Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon conrstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Rolling

  • 3 tablespoons (35g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (120g) confectioners' sugar

Instructions:

  1. Combine the flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar until creamed in a large bowl. Add the egg, lemon juice, lemon zest, and vanilla extract. Mix until combined.
  3. Add the flour mixture, mix until combined.
  4. Cover the dough and chill in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 350Â°F . Prepare baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  6. Scoop and roll about 1 tablespoon of dough to form a ball. Roll the dough ball first in the granulated sugar then generously in the confectioners' sugar. Place 3 inches (7cm) apart on the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-13 minutes in the preheated oven or until the edges start to turn a golden color.
  8. Cool for 5 minutes on a cooling rack.
  9. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
