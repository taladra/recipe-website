Classic Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, room temperature or softened slightly
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanila
  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar, for coating

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, milk and vanilla in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder and salt.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir until combined. The dough may seem thick, but it will come together. If dough is too dry, stir in an additional tablespoon of milk.
  5. Roll dough into rounded tablespoon sized balls and roll in sugar.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for about 10 minutes. They may not look quite done yet but they will set on the pan. Allow cookies to cool on the pan for at least 5 minutes before transferring to wire cooling rack.
  7. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
