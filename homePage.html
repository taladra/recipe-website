About Us


Piece of Cake Bakery was established in 2023 by Tasneem Aladra. Tasneem is a software engineering student who loves to bake in her free time. She decided to make this website to share some of her favorite recipies with those who have a love for baking just like her. If you also love to bake and are looking for simple yet absolutely delicious recipies to follow, then you are in the right place. The team here at Piece of Cake Bakery is extremely delighted to cater to all your baking needs.
