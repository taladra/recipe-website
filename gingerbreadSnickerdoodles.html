Gingerbread Snickerdoodle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar, for coating
  • 3/4 teaspoon gingerbread spice, for coating

Instructions:

  1. In a small microwave safe mixing bowl, melt the butter in 20 second intervals, stirring after each interval just until the butter has completely melted.
  2. Whisk the dark brown sugar into the melted butter. Then, vigorously whisk in the molasses and egg until well combined.
  3. Add in the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Use a rubber spatula to gently mix the flour into the dough just until no flour streaks remain.
  4. Cover the dough and transfer it to the fridge to rest and set up for at least 10 minutes, up to overnight.
  5. In the meantime, preheat the oven to 350Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the sugar and gingerbread spice.
  7. Use a 3 tbsp. cookie scoop to dish out the cookies. One at a time, place the cookie dough balls into the gingerbread spice sugar and gently move them around until they are fully covered in the sugar.
  8. Evenly space out the coated cookie dough balls on the lined baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
