Blueberry Streusel Muffins

Ingredients:

Streusel

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tspn ground cinnamon

Muffins

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tspn vanilla
  • 1/3 cup whole milk
  • 3 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 cup + 1 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1 tspn baking powder
  • 1/4 tspn baking soda
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 1/2 cup bluberries
  • 1/4 cup blueberry jam

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425° F and line a muffin pan with 6 parchment liners.

For the Streusel:

  1. In a small bowl, melt the butter. Add in the brown sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  2. Use a fork to mix the ingredients together, then set aside.

For the Muffins:

  1. In a medium sized mixing bowl melt the butter, Whisk in the sugar, followed by the egg, vanilla, sour cream and milk.
  2. Add in the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Use a rubber spatula to lightly fold the mixture until just a few flour streaks remain.
  3. Add in the blueberries and fold just until no flour streaks remain.
  4. Now it's time to layer the muffins. Start by pouring ~2 tbsp. of muffin batter into the bottom of each muffin liner. Top with 1 tsp. of blueberry jam, followed by ~1 tbsp. of streusel.
  5. Top with an additional ~3 tbsp. of muffin batter, 1 tsp. of blueberry jam, and the remaining streusel.
  6. Bake the muffins at 425°F for 5 minutes. Without opening the oven door, reduce the temperature to 350°F.
  7. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the muffins comes out clean.
  8. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
