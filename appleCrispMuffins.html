Apple Crisp Muffins

Ingredients:

Cinnamon Oat Streusel

  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 3/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 tspn ground cinnamon

Muffins

  • 1 medium sized apple
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 3.5 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp light brown sugar/li>
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tspn vanilla
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tspn baking powder
  • 1/2 tspn baking soda
  • 1/2 tspn ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tspn salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425° F and line a muffin pan with 6 parchment liners.
  2. For

For the Streusel:

  1. In a small bowl, melt the butter. Add in the flour, oats, sugar, and cinnamon.
  2. Use a fork to mix the ingredients together until you have formed little clumps of dough.

For the Muffins:

  1. Wash, peel, and dice the apple into small cubes.
  2. Melt the butter. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the melted butter and both sugars.
  3. Add in the egg, vanilla, milk, and sour cream, whisking until well combined.
  4. Add in the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. Use a rubber spatula to lightly fold the ingredients together just until a few flour streaks remain.
  5. Add in the diced apples, and continue to fold just until no flour streaks remain.
  6. Evenly distribute the muffin batter between the six parchment liners. Top each muffin generously (~2 tsp. / each) with the cinnamon oat streusel.
  7. Bake the muffins at 425°F for 5 minutes. Without opening the oven door, reduce the temperature to 350°F. Bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the muffins comes out clean.
  8. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
