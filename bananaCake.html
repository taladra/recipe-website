Banana Loaf Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 6 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 2-4 ripe bananas
  • 1 tbsp yoghurt
  • 2 tspn cinnamon
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tspn baking powder
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1 tspn salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â° F. Grease a loaf pan.
  2. Combine the butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl until well incorporated.
  3. Add in the eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Smash the bananas and add into the butter-sugar mixture. Add in the yoghurt.
  5. In a small mixing bowl combine the cinnamon, flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  6. Using a rubber spatula, mix half of the flour mixture into the wet ingredients until well combined. Mix in the rest of the flour mixture.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cake to cool in the loaf pan for 10-15 minutes before transferring to a wire rack for an additional 10 minutes of cooling.
  10. Enjoy your freshly baked treat!
